Places to go

Here are some of my favorite places to spend Christmas at:

  • United Kingdom
    London
  • Finland
    Lapland
  • Germany
    Cologne
  • Switzerland
    Zermatt
London

If you are looking for one of the best European cities to celebrate Christmas, then visit London. The Christmas lights and Christmas decorations are incredible. Also, the Christmas markets in Covent Garden or at Hyde Park Winter Wonderland are amazing.

  • Not-so-cold
Lapland

One of the best snowy winter vacations in Europe is Lapland, Finland, in the Arctic Circle. Between the months of November and March, the lakes freeze, the sun sets early and the sheer white snow can reach a depth of up to 35″ (90cm), so don’t forget your snow boots!

  • Real cold
Cologne

One of the best places to visit in Europe for the Christmas holidays has to be Cologne. This German city transforms into a dreamy winter wonderland at this time of year, with some of the best Christmas markets everywhere you turn!

  • Medium cold
Zermatt

One of the best places to visit in Europe for a snowy winter wonderland is the little mountain village of Zermatt in Switzerland. This lovely place is hidden in the end of a deep valley, in the very south of Switzerland, on the border to Italy.
